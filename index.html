About Us

Discover adventure with Bla Bla Sail. We offer sailing services and unforgettable experiences.

Two friends with a common passion: the sea. We are dedicated to create a unique experience, where adventure and relax are perfectly combined. We offer sailing trips in the beautiful coves of Sardinia, a dreamed place where nature unfolds in all its splendor.

On our services, you can enjoy the tranquility of the sea, swim in crystal clear waters, visit the best hidden islands and witness spectacular sunsets from the deck. We make sure that every outing is an opportunity to disconnect, relax, have fun and enjoy an unforgettable experience.
